About

My name is Xin Yang (pronounced as Sheen Young). I am currently a specially appointed researcher in the software engineering laboratory at Osaka University. I am originally from China, and I received my bachelor degree in software engineering from the Wuhan University of Technology in 2006. After that, I worked as a software engineer from 2006 to 2009 in China. I became a graduate student from 2011, I received my master degree (engineering) in 2013 and Ph.D. degree (engineering) in 2016. My research interests include empirical software engineering (EMSE), human and social aspects in SE, mining software repositories (MSR), and gamification in SE.
